sjgoodapps Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-28

This policy applies to apps published by sjgoodapps.

Data collection

sjgoodapps apps do not collect personal data from within the app for transmission to the developer, and sjgoodapps does not sell personal data. If a particular app ever introduces data collection beyond what is described here, this policy will be updated accordingly.

On-device processing

Many sjgoodapps apps process content, progress, preferences, worksheet generation, PDF creation, and language settings locally on your device.

On-device storage

sjgoodapps apps may store settings, progress, preferences, and related app state locally on your device so your setup can persist between sessions. Where an app creates documents such as PDFs, those files are generated on your device and remain there unless you choose to print, save, email, or share them.

Retention and deletion

Locally stored app data remains on your device until you remove it, reset it within the app if that option exists, or delete the app, subject to normal iOS backup and restore behavior. sjgoodapps does not maintain user accounts or server-side personal data for these apps unless explicitly stated in the app.

Third parties and remote services

sjgoodapps apps do not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, account systems, or cloud sync for their core features unless explicitly stated in the app. Some apps may fetch public reference content or open third-party websites, videos, or other external resources when you choose to use those features.

External links and public reference content

Where an app includes external links or public reference material, those third-party services may receive network information such as IP address and may handle your data under their own privacy policies. sjgoodapps is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services.

Email and sharing

If you choose to email, share, print, or open external resources from an app, that action happens only when you initiate it. Any information you include in an email is sent through your own mail account or the destination you choose.

Contacting support

If you contact support by email, the developer will receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. That information is used only to respond to your support request.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact sjgoodapps@gmail.com.
