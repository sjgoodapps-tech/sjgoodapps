sjgoodapps Support

Support for apps published by sjgoodapps, including setup, content, worksheet generation, printing, exporting, sharing, external links, and language settings.

If you need help, want to report a bug, or want to suggest an improvement, contact:

Email: sjgoodapps@gmail.com

We aim to respond within 3-5 business days.

Common support topics

  • App not starting, freezing, or closing unexpectedly
  • Worksheet settings, selected numbers or tables, question counts, or page size issues
  • Printing, saving, emailing, or sharing generated PDF worksheets
  • Study content, quizzes, audio, or progress-related issues in educational apps
  • Language selection and translated interface text
  • External links, public reference pages, or third-party video links not opening as expected
  • Generated filenames, export behavior, or document layout problems
  • Bug reports and feature suggestions

Helpful details to include

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The app version you are using
  • A short description of what happened
  • The screen, feature, or settings you were using when the issue happened
  • Screenshots if the issue is visual or layout-related

How the app works

sjgoodapps publishes educational and utility apps. Depending on the app, features may work entirely on-device or may include optional external links, public reference content, printable PDF generation, or local progress and preference storage.
